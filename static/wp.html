New album, merch, and series of shows!

February 23, 2015 Snackerjax Comments off


We will be releasing our second album “One” at Syntax Physic Opera this Friday 2/27! Muscle Brain and I Sank Molly Brown will be coming out to help us tear the house down.

We also have a a show at Deer Pile on the 13th of March, with local bad-asses Neptunian Weathervane in support of Venus and the Lion and Scarlet Cortex, both on tour from Santa Fe, NM.

On top of that, we are playing Altas‘ SXSW sendoff show on the 14th of March with Church Fire at the Hi-Dive!

We have an awesome crop of merch for sale at this run of shows. On top of CD’s and tapes, we have super comfortable t-shirts and v-necks, as well as hand numbered posters, all printed with art done by Jeanne Trueax from our upcoming album. We are also very busy writing and rehearsing material for our third record, if you decide to attend one of these shows there’s a good chance that you’ll catch a couple tunes that we’ve never played live before!

CORRECTIONS: Crash 45 on Saturday is FREE, show at Seventh Circle is on SUNDAY

December 5, 2013 The Bipedal Approach Comments off

The Approach is pleased to be mentioned by Westword’s Tom Murphy in his “The best DIY shows in Denver in December,” but unfortunately the date in the article is wrong — we’re playing at Seventh Circle on SUNDAY, 12/8. On Saturday, 12/7, we’ll be playing the Tinyamp Records/Literary Underground show at Crash 45, which we’ve just learned is FREE! But if you really want to spend some money, you can pick up a copy of Teeth Dreams there, or you can give it to us. We don’t mind, honestly. You might even get a t-shirt in return.

Back to back shows Saturday and Sunday!

Readers: Jason Hardung, Arielle Roberts, Dustin Holland, Brook Theis, William Seward Bonnie, Randall Chambers. This is also the release party for Teeth Dreams magazine!
